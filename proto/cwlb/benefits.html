COVID-19 benefits for individuals

CRA-administered benefits that provide temporary income support to people during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Services and information

Canada Recovery Caregiving Benefit (CRCB)

Income support if you must stop work to care for dependants due to closures, high risk and caregiver availability.

Canada Recovery Sickness Benefit (CRSB)

Up to 6 weeks of support if you are sick or must self-isolate due to COVID-19.

Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit (CWLB)

Income support for employed or self-employed individuals who cannot work during a designated regional COVID-19 lockdown.

Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB) Ended

The CRB has now ended. The CRA will continue to accept and process retroactive applications until December 22, 2021.
