Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit

The Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit gives income support to employed and self-employed individuals whose place of work is in a designated COVID-19 lockdown region. The CWLB is administered by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

If you are eligible for the CWLB, you can receive $300 ($270 after taxes withheld) for each 1-week period.

The benefit is available between October 24, 2021 and May 7, 2022. You will need to apply for each eligible 1-week period during the lockdown.
