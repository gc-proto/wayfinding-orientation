Before you apply

The easiest way to apply will be online through CRA My Account. You can get ready by answering these questions.

File your income taxes

  • No

When you can apply

You may start applying on the first Monday after the 1-week period you're applying for has ended. Applications do not renew automatically.

You must apply for each period separately. You may apply for any period you are eligible for that is open for application, including up to 60 days after the period has ended. The first 5 periods will remain open for applications until January 26, 2022.
