Contact us about CWLB

If you are calling to find out if your region is eligible for the benefit, contact Service Canada.

Online:

Use your CRA My Account to:

  • apply for the Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit (CWLB)
  • update your address and direct deposit information
Sign in to CRA My Account Register
By phone:

Contact the CRA by phone or TTY if you have:

  • general questions about the benefit
  • specific questions about your CRA account
Before you call:

To verify your identity, you'll need your:

  • Social Insurance Number (SIN)
  • full name and date of birth
  • complete address
  • assessed tax return, notice of assessment or reassessment, other tax document, or be signed in to CRA My Account
