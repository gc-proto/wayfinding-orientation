Weeks available for designated regions

Designated COVID-19 lockdown regions are only eligible for specific weeks. You must also check if you meet all other eligibility criteria before applying.

If you are eligible, you may apply for any 1-week period for up to 60 days after that period has ended.

Use the filter to find out if there are any periods you can apply for.

Filter by:

  • the first 3 characters of your postal code
  • the name of your region
  • your city
  • your province or territory
