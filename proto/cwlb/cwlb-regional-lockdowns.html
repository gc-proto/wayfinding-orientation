Available designated CWLB regions

Only designated COVID-19 lockdown regions are eligible for the CWLB. Use the filter to find out if there are any weeks you can apply for.

You may apply for any eligible 1-week period for up to 60 days after that period has ended.

Filter by:

  • the first 3 characters of your postal code
  • the name of your region
  • your city
  • your province or territory

If your region is not listed

Your region may be either:

Not eligible: Option 1 of 2

Your region may not be eligible if it is not listed. You cannot apply for the CWLB.

Not available yet: Option 2 of 2

Applications may not be available yet if the lockdown was only recently declared.

Check the list of designated CWLB regions and periods to confirm if your region is eligible.

If you have questions, need to find out what region you are in, or don’t know if your region is eligible, call Service Canada at 1-800-206-7218.
