How eligibility periods work

Each Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit (CWLB) eligibility period is a specific 1-week period. You may apply for any weeks your region is eligible between October 24, 2021 and May 7, 2022.

If you meet all the eligibility criteria, you can apply on the Monday after the first week your region is in a lockdown. You can only apply when a COVID-19 lockdown order is designated for your region.

Apply for each week separately

The CWLB does not renew automatically. You must apply for each 1-week period separately. The application process is the same each time.

Number of weeks you can apply for

There is no set maximum for the CWLB. The government decides the number of weeks you can apply for each lockdown. If you are still eligible, you may apply for all open periods during the designated lockdown in your region.

You may apply for any eligible 1-week period for up to 60 days after that period has ended.

You may apply for the first [6] periods until [February 2, 2022] if you are eligible.

Period start and end dates

Each 1-week period starts on a Sunday and ends on the following Saturday.
