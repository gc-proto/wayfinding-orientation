How much you can get

The Canada Worker Lockdown Benefit (CWLB) provides a payment for each 1-week period your region, province or territory is under a public health lockdown due to COVID-19. Only a government authority can designate a lockdown region.

If you're eligible, you may apply for all eligible weeks your region remains under a COVID-19 lockdown between October 24, 2021 and May 7, 2022.

How much you receive per period

If you are eligible, you will receive a payment of $300 (before taxes withheld) for each 1-week period you apply for the CWLB.

The CRA withholds 10% tax at source, so the actual payment you get is $270 per 1-week period.

How we calculate your payment

  • $300 (CWLB gross amount)
  • minus   $30 (10% tax withheld at source)
  • equals $270 (amount you receive per period)
