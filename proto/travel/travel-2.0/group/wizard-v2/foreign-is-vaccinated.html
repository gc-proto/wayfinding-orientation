No exemptions for vaccinated travellers

At this time, there are no exemptions to entry restrictions, quarantine or testing specifically for vaccinated travellers.

Studying transmission after vaccination

It has only been a few months since people around the world began to be vaccinated against COVID-19.

There is much that is not yet known about transmission of the virus by vaccinated people. The Government of Canada is studying the evidence about transmission as it becomes available.

As our knowledge changes, our policies to keep Canadians safe will change too.

The World Health Organization also recommends that vaccinated travellers shouldn't be exempted from entry and quarantine requirements at this time.
