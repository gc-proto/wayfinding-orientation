Assess your quarantine plan - Not suitable

You selected that you:

  • don't have a place to quarantine for 14 days or longer
  • or that you won't have access to basic necessities without leaving your place of quarantine

Based on the information you provided, your place of quarantine is not suitable.

For a quarantine plan to be suitable, your place of quarantine must be a location where you can stay for 14 days or longer, and have access to the necessities of life including food, water, medication and heat without leaving your place of quarantine.

What you need to do

Make alternate arrangements

You won't likely be allowed to quarantine at this location. Please make alternate arrangements within your means. This could include a hotel or other suitable location.

Make your quarantine plan

If a government representative at the port of entry determines that your quarantine plan is not suitable, you will be required to stay in a PHAC approved facility for the 14-day mandatory quarantine period at your expense. [wording to be confirmed with Cabinet approval of plan]
