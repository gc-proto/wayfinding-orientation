Who can come to Canada - Citizen with symptoms

You indicated that:

  • you are a Canadian citizen, a permanent resident, a person registered under the Indian Act or a protected person
  • you are showing symptoms consistent with COVID-19

Based on your answers, you can enter Canada by land or by sea.

If you have symptoms, you won't be able to board a flight to come to Canada.

You also must follow a mandatory isolation period of 14 days.
