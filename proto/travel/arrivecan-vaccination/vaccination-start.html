Vaccination status

Collecting Covid-19 vaccination data

This is a working prototype of how questions about a traveller's vaccination status may be asked in ArriveCAN. Travellers entering Canada must use ArriveCAN to submit their travel details, quarantine plan and soon, vaccination status. Travellers show their ArriveCAN receipt at the border crossing.

Please give the questions a try on your phone or computer, whichever you are more likely to use for ArriveCAN. There is an opportunity at the end to provide feedback. We're looking for problems people might experience so we can be ready with answers when vaccine exemptions become available.
