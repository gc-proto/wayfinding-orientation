Mandatory 14-day quarantine or isolation

Everyone entering Canada must follow Mandatory isolation or quarantine requirements.

Pre-departure testing will soon be required for all people flying into Canada

All air travellers will be required to test negative for COVID-19 before boarding interntional flights bound for Canada.
