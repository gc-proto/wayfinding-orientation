Compare the claim methods

For your 2020 tax return, there are two options:

  • Temporary flat rate method
  • Detailed method

Temporary flat rate method

Applies to:

  • employees working from home due to the COVID-19 pandemic

With this method:

  • you can claim $2 for each day you worked from home, up to a maximum of $400
  • your employer isn't required to complete Form T2200
  • you're not required to keep documents to support your claim

Detailed method

Applies to:

  • employees working from home due to the COVID-19 pandemic
  • employees working from home for any other reason

With this method:

  • you can claim the actual amounts you paid, supported by receipts, bills and Form T2200S/T2200

If you are self-employed, refer to: Business-use-of-home expenses
