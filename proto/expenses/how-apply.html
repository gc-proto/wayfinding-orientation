Flat rate claim of $x

If you are claiming $x or less in work space and office supply expenses, you can use the quick claim method. You are not required to provide receipts.

Tip: Use the calculator to determine if you are entitled to a higher claim. A claim more than $x requires you to follow the standard claim steps.

  1. Confirm you are eligible

    You must meet the eligibility criteria to claim your expenses.

  2. Claim the deduction on your tax return

    Claim $x the amount on Line 22900 - Other employment expenses on your tax return

Standard claim

  1. Confirm you are eligible

    You must meet the eligibility criteria to claim your expenses.

  2. Fill in the form

    Tip: Use the calculator to determine your personal-use expenses and work space use to claim on the T777 Short or T777.

    Form for those working from home due to COVID-19: Option 1 of 2

    Use the Form T777 Short - Statement of Employment Expenses for Working at Home Due to COVID-19, if you are only claiming expenses related to working from home because of COVID-19.

    Standard form: Option 2 of 2

    Use the Form T777 - Statement of Employment Expenses if you need to include additional expenses like office supplies, gas, vehicle, parking, etc.

  3. Claim the deduction on your tax return

    Claim the amount on Line 22900 - Other employment expenses on your tax return
