Question about your eligible employees

1. Did you have any eligible employees working for you during the claim period?

Which employees are eligible

An eligible employee is a person who was employed primarily in Canada during the portion of the claim period in which you employed them. They must have actively worked for at least part of the claim period.

Employee eligibility depends on the person being employed in Canada, not living in Canada.
