Canada Recovery Hiring Program (CRHP)

As a Canadian employer who continues to be impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic, you may be eligible to apply for a subsidy to cover part of your wages as you hire new employees and increase existing employees' wages or hours.

Each claim period, eligible employers can claim either the CRHP, or the wage portion of the Tourism and Hospitality Recovery Program (THRP) or the Hardest-Hit Businesses Recovery Program (HHBRP), whichever provides the highest amount.

Before October 24, 2021, eligible employers could claim either the CRHP or the Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy (CEWS).

Wage and hiring

The wage or hiring subsidy will enable you to re-hire workers previously laid off as a result of COVID-19, help prevent further job losses, and better position you to resume normal operations following the crisis.
