Canada Emergency Rent Subsidy (CERS)

As a Canadian business, charity, or non-profit who was affected by the COVID-19 pandemic between September 27, 2020, and October 23, 2021, you may have been eligible for the CERS.

Beginning October 24, 2021, the CERS was replaced with two new programs offering rent and wage support:

  • the Tourism and Hospitality Recovery Program
  • the Hardest-Hit Businesses Recovery Program

If you are eligible for the CERS, you can still apply for past claim periods until 180 days after the claim period has ended.

Rent and property

The rent subsidy covers part of commercial rent or property expenses and will be provided directly to the qualifying renter or property owner, not the landlord.

Wage and hiring

The wage or hiring subsidy will enable you to re-hire workers previously laid off as a result of COVID-19, help prevent further job losses, and better position you to resume normal operations following the crisis.
