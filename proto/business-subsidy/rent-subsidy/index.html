COVID-19 rent and property expense support for businesses

As a Canadian business, non-profit organization, or charity who has seen a drop in revenue during the COVID-19 pandemic, you may be eligible for a subsidy to cover part of your commercial rent or property expenses.

Depending on the period you are applying for, you may be eligible to claim 1 of the following:

  • Tourism and Hospitality Recovery Program (THRP) – Rent
    Effective: October 24, 2021, to May 2022
  • Hardest-Hit Businesses Recovery Program (HHBRP) – Rent
    Effective: October 24, 2021, to May 2022
  • Canada Emergency Rent Subsidy (CERS)
    Effective: September 27, 2020, to October 23, 2021

Most of the rules for eligible expenses, qualifying properties, and how revenue is calculated are the same as they were before October 24, 2021.

If you are eligible for a base rent subsidy, you may also be eligible for a lockdown support top-up if your business location is significantly affected by a public health order for a week or more.
