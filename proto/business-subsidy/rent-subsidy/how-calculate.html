How much you may claim

The rent subsidies provide a base subsidy amount and in some cases, a lockdown support top-up amount.

In each claim period, you may claim eligible expenses up to certain maximum amounts:

Maximum eligible expense CERS THRP HHBRP
Per business location
(Base subsidy plus lockdown support top-up		 $75,000 $75,000 $75,000
Total for all locations
Applies to base subsidy only. There is no maximum for the lockdown support top-up		 $300,000 $1,000,000 $1,000,000

If you were affected by a COVID-19-related public health order, you may also qualify for a lockdown support top-up of 25% of eligible expenses for each affected location for the days the order was in place.

Calculating your base rent subsidy rate

Your base rent subsidy rate is the percentage of eligible expenses you may claim for a claim period.

The amount you can claim is based on the revenue drop you experienced between your revenue in the current claim period and a prior reference period.

There are 2 prior reference period options for calculating your revenue drop:

General prior reference period

The eligible revenue you earned in the corresponding month of the previous year

or

Alternative prior reference period

The average of the eligible revenue you earned in January and February 2020
