Local Lockdown Program

The Local Lockdown Program is part of the Tourism and Hospitality Recovery Program (THRP). It is a way businesses, charities, and non-profits affected by a local lockdown resulting from a qualifying public health restriction can qualify for wage and rent support through the THRP, even if they are not part of the tourism or hospitality sectors.

This subsidy will be available to all businesses, within any business sector. You may apply under the Tourism and Hospitality Recovery Program (THRP).
