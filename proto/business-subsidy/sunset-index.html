Managing your business during COVID-19

COVID-19 wage and rent subsidies for businesses

Status: All subsidies are closed

The CRA administered COVID-19 subsidies to temporarily support wage and rent expenses for businesses, charities, and non-profits in Canada between March 15, 2020, and May 7, 2022.

As of November 4, 2022, you can no longer apply for these subsidies.
