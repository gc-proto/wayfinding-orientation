Managing your business during COVID-19

Wage and rent subsidies

As a Canadian business, charity, or non-profit affected by the COVID-19 pandemic, you may be eligible to apply for one or more subsidies to cover part of your wage and rent expenses.

Answer a few questions to see what you qualify for

Types of subsidies

Wage and hiring

Cover part of your employee payroll based on your drop in revenue during the COVID-19 pandemic

Rent and property

Cover part of your commercial rent or property expenses based on your drop in revenue during the COVID-19 pandemic

Current and past subsidy programs

Canada Recovery Hiring Program (CRHP)

Support for increasing wage and hiring expenses as business resumes and the economy recovers

Tourism and Hospitality Recovery Program (THRP) New

Targeted wage and rent subsidy support for eligible organizations in the tourism and hospitality industry or deeply affected by a current public health lockdown

Hardest-Hit Business Recovery Program (HHBRP) New

Support for hard-hit eligible organizations that have been deeply affected since the start of the pandemic

Local Lockdown Program (LLP) New

Targeted wage and rent subsidy support for eligible organizations in all sectors deeply affected by a current public health lockdown

Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy (CEWS)

Wage subsidy for eligible employers from March 15, 2020, to October 23, 2021

Canada Emergency Rent Subsidy (CERS)

Rent subsidy for eligible businesses, charities and non-profits from September 27, 2020, to October 23, 2021

10% Temporary Wage Subsidy for Employers (TWS) Closed

Wage subsidy for qualifying businesses from March 18, 2020, to June 19, 2020
