Eligible entities for Tourism and Hospitality Recovery Program (THRP)

Introduction statement/overview here

List of eligible activities

Airports

Activities that involve airports, such as:

  • operating or managing an airport
  • renting hangar space
  • baggage handling
  • cargo handling
  • aircraft parking services

Amusement and theme parks

Activities that involve amusement and theme parks, such as:

  • managing a park, which inclues:
    • operating a variety of attractions, such as mechanical rides, water rides, games, shows or theme exhibits
    • leasing space on a concession basis for these operations
  • operation and maintenance of non-gambling coin-operated amusement devices located in a business operated by others

Amusement arcades

Activities that involve operating or managing an amusement arcade, such as:

  • family fun centre
  • indoor play area
  • pinball arcade
  • video game arcade

Camping and mobile homes

Not eligible

A business not eligible as operating or managing serviced or unserviced sites to accommodate campers and their equipment, such as:

  • mobile home sites

Activities that involve camping and mobile homes, such as:

  • operating or managing serviced or unserviced sites to accommodate campers and their equipment for:
    • tents
    • tent trailers
    • travel trailers
    • recreational vehicles

Camps

Activities that involve operating or managing an camp, such as:

  • children's camp (overnight)
  • family vacation camp (overnight)
  • outdoor adventure retreat (overnight)
  • hunting camp
  • fishing camp

Casinos

Activities that include casinos, such as:

  • operating or managing a casino

Charter bus services

Activities that involve charter bus service, such as:

  • providing charter bus services, if the:
    • buses do not operate over fixed routes and schedules
    • entire vehicle is rented, rather than individual seats

Cinemas

Activities that involve operating or managing a facilitythat is primarily engaged in exhibiting motion pictures, such as:

  • cinema
  • drive-in theatre

Cruise ship platforms

Actitivies that involve cruise ship platforms, such as:

  • operating a facility allowing passengers to board and leave a cruise ship

Cultural and artistic performances

Activities that involve organizing, promoting, hosting, supporting or participating in events that meet the artistic or cultural interests of their patrons, such as:

  • live performancepromote, artistic, cultural
  • exhibits intended for public viewingpromote, artistic, cultural

Duty-free retail stores

Activities that involve duty-free stores, such as:

  • operating or managing a duty-free retail store at a land border crossing where:
    • the only exit route is to the United States

Hotels and short-term lodging

Activities that involve operating or managing a facility providing short-term lodging, such as:

  • hotelshort-term lodging
  • motelshort-term lodging
  • cottageshort-term lodging
  • bed and breakfastshort-term lodging
  • hostelshort-term lodging

Museums and cultural sites

Activities that involve operating, preserving and exhibiting objects, sites and natural wonders of historical, cultural and educational value, such as:

  • museum
  • historic and heritage site
  • zoo
  • botanical garden
  • nature park

Promotions

Activities that involve promotions, such as:

  • promoting the interests of the members of an organization that belong to the tourism, hospitality, arts, entertainment and recreation industry

Recreational facilities

Activities that involve operating or managing a facility or providing services that enable patrons to participate in recreational activities, such as:

  • downhill and cross-country ski/snowboard areas, including:
    • equipment rental service
    • ski/snowboard instruction service provided at the area
  • operation of docking and storage facility for pleasure-craft owners, with or without related activities, such as:
    • retailing fuel and marine supplies
    • boat repair and maintenance
    • rental service
  • fitness and recreational sports centre
  • amateur sports club
  • teams or leagues
  • archery or shooting
  • ballroom dancing club
  • river rafting club
  • curling clubs
  • mini golf and bowling

Not eligible

A business not eligible as operating or managing a facility or providing services that enable patrons to participate in recreational activities, such as:

  • golf
  • golf instructions
  • golf course (country club)
  • golf driving range
  • golf clubhouse
  • professional sports clubs, teams or leagues
  • facilities used primarily by professional sports clubs, teams or leagues

Restaurants and food or drink

Activities that involve preparing and serving meals, snacks and beverages made to order for immediate consumption on or off the premises, such as:

  • restaurantfood, drink, beverage
  • food truckfood, drink, beverage
  • cafeteriafood, drink, beverage
  • cateringfood, drink, beverage
  • coffee shopfood, drink, beverage
  • food concessionfood, drink, beverage
  • barfood, drink, beverage
  • pubfood, drink, beverage
  • nightclubfood, drink, beverage

Not eligible

A facility primarily engaged in retailing food or beverage product, such as:

  • supermarket (grocery store) food, drink, beverage
  • convenience (corner) storefood, drink, beverage

Sightseeing and scenic tours

Activities that involve organizing, promoting or supporting scenic  and sightseeing tours, such as:

  • sightseeing or dinner cruise
  • steam train excursion
  • horse-drawn sightseeing ride
  • air-boat ride
  • hot-air balloon ride
  • charter fishing service

Travel agencies and tour operators

Activities that involve operating a travel agency or as a tour operator, such as:

  • agent for tour operatorstravel agency, tour operator
  • transporation companies in selling traveltravel agency, tour operator
  • short-term lodging establishments in selling traveltravel agency, tour operator
  • tour and accommodation servicestravel agency, tour operator

Not eligible

A business not eligible as operating as a travel agency or as a tour operator, such as:

  • arranging, assembling and marketing tourstravel agency, tour operator

Tourism promotion

Activities that involve turism promotion, such as:

  • promoting a destination or region in Canada for the purpose of attracting tourism

Trade shows, festivals, weddings

Activities that involve organizing, promoting, hosting or supporting events such as:
