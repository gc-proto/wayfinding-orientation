Canada Emergency Student Benefit (CESB)

Protect yourself against cyber incidents, fraud and identity theft

Help protect your CRA account and online information:

  • Use unique passwords for all your accounts
  • Sign up for CRA account email notifications
  • Set up a Personal Identification Number (PIN) to use when you call the CRA
  • Keep your contact information current

For more tips, go to Security of your CRA account.

If you notice any suspicious account activity, contact the CRA to report a security incident.

The Canada Emergency Student Benefit (CESB) provides financial support to post-secondary students, and recent post-secondary and high school graduates who are unable to find work due to COVID-19.

This benefit is for students who do not qualify for the Canada Emergency Response Benefit (CERB) or Employment Insurance (EI).

From May to August 2020, the CESB provides a payment to eligible students of:

$1,250
For each 4-week period

or

$2,000
For each 4-week period, if you have dependants or a disability

All applications for the CESB must be submitted before September 30, 2020.
