You may need to notify us

You don't need premarket authorization to sell the product, but you may need to notify us before sale, depending on the product:

Foods for very low evergy diets

Foods for very low energy diets require premarket notification at least 90 days before sale, even if they comply with the applicable regulations.

Notifying Health Canada for foods for very low energy diets

Other foods for special dietary use

Foods for special dietary use (other than foods for very low energy diets) don't require premarket review or authorization prior to sale in Canada.

For more information, you can contact the Bureau of Nutritional Sciences via email at hc.bns-bsn.sc@canada.ca.
