How much you can get

The Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB) provides benefit payments for each 2-week period you apply for between September 27, 2020 and October 23, 2021. New

The CRA withholds a 10% tax at source on each payment you receive.

How much you receive per period

If you’re eligible, the CRB is available for a maximum of 27 periods (54 weeks). New

You can only apply for periods that are open for application.

If you applied for CRB periods before July 18, 2021

Your CRB amount will reduce after you receive 21 payments.

Your first 21 payments: Option 1 of 2

$1,000 ($900 after taxes withheld) for each 2-week period until you reach 21 periods (42 weeks) or the CRB ends

After 21 payments: Option 2 of 2

$600 ($540 after taxes withheld) for each 2-week period until you reach the maximum 27 periods (54 weeks) or the CRB ends

If your first CRB period starts after July 17, 2021

You may receive $600 ($540 after taxes withheld) for each 2-week period until the CRB ends.

Changes to CRB amounts for period 22

Your CRB amount may change based on how many periods you've applied for, or if your first time applying for the CRB is for period 22 (July 18 to 31, 2021) or a later period.
