Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB)

The proposed Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB) would give income support to employed and self-employed individuals whose income is directly affected by COVID-19. The benefit is intended to assist people who are ready to work, and looking for work, but do not qualify for the new Employment Insurance (EI) benefits.

The CRB would provide $1,000 (before taxes withheld) per 2-week period, for up to 26 weeks.

The CRB is subject to Parliamentary approval. Information about eligibility and how to apply will be available in the coming weeks.

EI or Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB)

  1. First, determine if you qualify for Employment Insurance (EI)

    Service Canada is temporarily changing the EI rules to allow more people affected by COVID-19 to qualify.

    You may get a minimum rate of $400 per week for up to 26 weeks.

    Review highlights of EI changes
    • You will now only need a minimum of 120 insurable hours (in which you paid into EI) for EI regular benefits
    • You may get a minimum rate of $400 per week for up to 26 weeks
    • You can still work, but your benefit will reduce by $0.50 for every dollar you earn (up to 90% of previous weekly earning)
    • If you qualify for EI, you may apply for it as of September 27, 2020

  2. Second, if you don’t qualify for EI, the CRB may be right for you

    Applications for the CRB have not opened. But you can get ready to apply.

    Review proposed eligibility criteria

    The benefit would be available to residents in Canada who:

    • are at least 15 years old and have a valid Social Insurance Number (SIN)
    • have stopped working due to the COVID-19 pandemic and are available and looking for work, or are working and have had a reduction in their employment or self-employment income for reasons related to COVID-19
    • are not eligible for EI
    • had employment or self-employment income of at least $5,000 in 2019 or in 2020
    • have not quit their job voluntarily

    This benefit is subject to Parliametary approval. More information will be available in the coming weeks.

Caregiving and sickness

If neither EI not the CRB are right for you, one of the other recovery benefits may apply to your situation:

  • Canada Recovery Caregiving Benefit (CRCB)
  • Canada Recovery Sickness Benefit (CRSB)

You may only apply for one Canada recovery benefit at a time for the same period.
