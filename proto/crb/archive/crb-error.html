If you get an error when you apply

Some applicants may get an error code when they apply online for the CRB. Follow the instructions in the error message to resolve the issue.

On this page

  • Further validation
  • Conflict with EI claim

Further validation

The CRA verifies every CRB application. But if your application requires further validation, it may be because information is missing or we need additional documentation to confirm your eligibility.

If your application needs validation

Conflict with EI claim

If you have an open EI claim with Service Canada, it can conflict with your CRB application. Even if you're no longer receiving EI payments, a previous claim that is still open will prevent you from being eligible for the CRB.

What you can do:

  1. Find out if you have an open EI claim with Service Canada:

    Sign in to My Service Canada Account (MSCA): Option 1 of 2

    Using GCKey or your bank

    Contact Service Canada (EI): Option 2 of 2

    By phone, by mail, or in person

    If you don't have an open EI claim, you should be able to apply for the CRB. Check that you meet all the eligibility criteria.

  2. Ask Service Canada if you're eligible for EI regular benefits or what you must do to become eligible

  3. If you're not currently eligible for EI and there is no way to become eligible, ask Service Canada to close your open EI claim

  4. After your EI claim is closed, return to the CRB eligibility criteria list to make sure you meet all the other criteria
