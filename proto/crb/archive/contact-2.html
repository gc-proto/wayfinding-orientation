Contact us about CRB

Online:

Use your CRA My Account to:

  • apply for the Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB)
  • update your address and direct deposit information
Sign in to CRA My Account Register
By phone:

What do you want to call about?

  • You want general information about the benefit
  • You have questions about your CRA account information
  • You want to report a security incident

Call the recovery benefit line for general questions about

  • CRB eligibility requirements
  • Payments amount for each period
  • how and when to apply

This line is for general benefit information only. For specifics about your account, select a different option above.

Call the CRA for questions about your account information, including if you:

  • have trouble applying for the CRB because you haven’t filed a tax return recently
  • want to make changes to your account (updates to address or direct deposit)
  • have trouble accessing CRA My Account
Before you call:

To verify your identity, you'll need your:

  • Social Insurance Number (SIN)
  • full name and date of birth
  • complete address
  • assessed return, notice of assessment or reassessment, other tax document, or be signed in to CRA My Account

If you suspect you’re a victim of fraud, identity theft, or a cyber incident
Call the CRA:

If you notice changes or suspicious activity on your account, contact the CRA at: 1-800-959-8281 (opens up phone application)1-800-959-8281

Select the option “report suspected fraud or identity theft” to prioritize your call.

or

Request a call back:

If you cannot wait on the phone, you can ask the CRA to call you back. To set up a call back, go to Need assistance.

If your Social Insurance Number (SIN) has been stolen

Contact Service Canada at: 1-866-274-6627 (opens up phone application)1-866-274-6627

If you have concerns about a call or email you received about your CESB payment

Contact the Canadian Anti-fraud Centre at: 1-888-495-8501 (opens up phone application)1-888-495-8501
