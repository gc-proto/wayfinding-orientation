Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB) - Closed

Status: Closed

The CRB closed to retroactive applications on December 22, 2020. You can no longer apply for this benefit.

The Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB) gave income support to employed and self-employed individuals who are directly affected by COVID-19 and were not entitled to Employment Insurance (EI) benefits. The CRB was administered by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

Depending on when you started applying for the CRB, you could have either receive $1,000 ($900 after taxes withheld) or $600 ($540 after taxes withheld) for a 2-week period.
