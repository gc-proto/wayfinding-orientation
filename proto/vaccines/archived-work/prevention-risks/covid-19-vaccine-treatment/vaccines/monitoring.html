Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine: Continued monitoring

  • What you should know
  • Authorization information
  • Continued monitoring

Vaccines on the market in Canada are monitored continuously. Commonly reported side effects of vaccination, such as pain at the site of injection, body chills, feeling tired or feverish are not considered safety concerns to the health of Canadians. At the time of authorization, no major safety concerns have been reported in the available data submitted to Health Canada.

We will take prompt action should any safety concerns arise. This could include warning Canadians about potential side effects (adverse events), changing the recommended use of the product or even removing the product from the market.

Updates on post-market activities

Health Canada will provide updates on this page when further safety information is provided to us by the manufacturer or other sources.
