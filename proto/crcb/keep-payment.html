Keep getting your payments

On this page

  • You must re-apply every week
  • When to expect your payment

You must re-apply every week

The CRCB provides a maximum of 26 payments and does not renew automatically. To keep getting your payments, you must re-apply after every period.

To confirm the number of periods you have already received the CRCB, you can review your application history in CRA My Account, under “COVID-19 Support Application Details”.

Make sure you're still eligible

You must continue to meet all the CRCB eligibility criteria to keep receiving the benefit. Check that you are still eligible every time you re-apply.

How to re-apply

You may only apply after the 1-week period you are applying for has ended. The application process is the same every time.

Determine when you can apply.

If you know when to apply, apply online or by phone.

When to expect your payment

If you are eligible for the CRCB, you can expect to get $500 ($450 after taxes withheld) for the 1-week period you applied for.

Normal processing time

Direct deposit
Direct deposit takes 5 business days if you have it set up with the CRA.
By mail
A mailed cheque should arrive in about 14 business days.

If further validation is needed

If your application requires further validation, processing may take an additional 4 to 6 weeks.
