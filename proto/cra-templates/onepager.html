We may validate your application before issuing your payment

The CRA is validating applications in order to maintain public confidence in Canada's tax system and ensure that payments are available for people who need them most.

If you have to validate your application, call the toll-free number provided.

Before you call, gather the following items

Information you need on hand:

  • Social Insurance Number (SIN)
  • Full name and date of birth
  • A government issued identification such as a driver's license, health card, or passport
  • Access to your CRA My Account, if applicable, or an assessed return, notice of assessment or reassessment, or other tax document

Documents you may need if applicable:

  • Proof of residency (household bills, insurance policies, rental agreement, driver’s license)
  • 2 pieces of government issued identification
  • Documents showing how COVID-19 has affected your employment income such as:
    • recent pay slips
    • record of employment (ROE)
    • bank statements showing name, address, and payroll deposit
    • Invoices for services rendered (for self-employed or sub-contractors)
    • payment receipts for services provided (for self-employed or sub-contractors)
  • Employer name and address
  • Statements of any other benefits you receive
