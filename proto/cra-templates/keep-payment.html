How to re-apply

To keep receiving the CERB, you must apply again for each period. The application process is the same every time.

Determine When to apply

If you know when to apply, apply online or by phone

The CRA will verify that you are eligible to receive the [ABC]. If you received a [ABC] payment that you are not eligible for, we will contact you to make arrangements to repay any amounts you may owe. Canadians can also report suspected [ABC] misuse through the CRA’s Leads program.

When and how to expect your payment

If you are eligible for the CERB, you can expect to get $2,000 ($500 per week) for the 4-week eligibility period you applied for.

Direct deposit

Direct deposit takes 3 business days if you have it set up with the CRA.

By mail

A mailed cheque should arrive in about 10 business days.
