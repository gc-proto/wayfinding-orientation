Eligibility criteria

To be eligible for the [Name of X] [ABC], you must meet the following conditions:

  • You can only get 1 emergency benefit at a time

    You are only allowed to receive one type of federal emergency income support benefit in the same week: the CERB, the [YYY], the [ZZZ], the [WWW], or EI benefits.

  • Find the right benefit for you

    If you finished your CERB payments and still need financial support, find out which emergency support benefit is the best one for you.

    Go to: What to do after CERB

    What counts towards the $5,000?
    • All employment or self-employment income, tips, non-eligible dividends, honoraria, and royalties
    • Amounts you may get from other emergency benefits (CERB, CESB, the [YYY], the [ZZZ] and the [WWW]) do not count as income

You need all of the above to be eligible for the [ABC].

Verifying your eligibility

The CRA will be verifying that you are eligible to receive the [ABC]. You may receive a notice from the CRA asking you to validate your application. You will need to contact the CRA to verify your information and process your application.

If you received a payment and are later found to be ineligible, we will contact you to make arrangments to repay any amounts owing.

Canadians can also report suspected misuse through the CRA's Leads program.
